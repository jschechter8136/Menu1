Our Story

Our restaurant originally started out as a small ice cream shop called Bailey's creamery in Downtown St. Louis. Bailey's creamery was founded by the Bailey family in 1984. Janet and Phil Bailey always dreamed of opening an ice cream parlor together and when the couple married in 1984, their dream became a reality. Bailey's creamery soon became a St. Louis favorite. During the time Bailey's creamery was in business, they remained classily dedicated to family ideals, community, and the simplicity of their five original flavors. All of the creamery's ice cream was made from local and pure cow milk. When their son David finished his business degree, he took over the family business and relocated and expanded the creamery. The name was changed to Bailey's Range and was transformed into a full restaurant. The restaurant's menu has greatly expanded over the years, however has stayed true to the original values of family, community, and simplicity.
